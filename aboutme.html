About Me

Hi, I'm ComatoseHuman (Robin Tomos), and I'm quite interested in tech art. I really enjoy developing innovative pipelines and creating systems that allow artists to create cooler things, faster. So far this involves 3D asset manipulation, developing efficient tileset production pipelines and data manipulation to allow artists to work in the environment they find most comfortable. I have experience in Blender, Houdini, Substance Designer and Python, and am currently learning Maya.
